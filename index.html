Current project: Nonlinear control

Control of nonlinear systems plays a crucial role in many practical systems. Most of the real systems are nonlinear, then performs control of nonlinear systems are such important.

There are many challenges, for example:

Landing a ship on high seas. The control problem: to track a motion consisting of a superposition of oscillations of unknown frequency, amplitude, and phase despite large model uncertainties.

Floating offshore wind turbines. A 100m tall wind tower supporting a 5MW turbine is no visible if at 35km from the shore. At this distance, stronger and steadier wind prevails. But water depths prevent floor-mounted twoers: floating towers are the solution. The control problem: to reject a disturbance consisting of a superposition of oscillations of unknown frequency, amplitude and phase despite large model uncertainties.
